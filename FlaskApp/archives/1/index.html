  • ABOUT

    Mount Vernon Nazarene University, established by the Church of the Nazarene, opened its doors in 1968 and serves the denomination’s East Central Region. The University offers liberal arts, professional, and graduate education at its main campus in Mount Vernon, Ohio as well as at multiple regional campuses.

MVNU NEWS

MVNU Goliards to sing at Quarry Chapel

Updated Apr 15 2023

MOUNT VERNON, Ohio (April 15, 2023) —Mount Vernon Nazarene University’s Goliards Chamber Choir will present a spring concert on Saturday, April 22, at 11 a.m., in the Quarry Chapel, 10930 Quarry Road, Gambier, Ohio. The concert is free and open to the public.

MVNU ensembles perform April 29

Updated Apr 15 2023

MOUNT VERNON, Ohio (April 15, 2023) —The Music Department at Mount Vernon Nazarene University will present the final concert of the year on Saturday, April 29, at 3 p.m. in the R.R. Hodges Chapel Auditorium. The concert is free and open to the public.

MVNU graphic design seniors featured at Schnormeier Gallery

Updated Apr 07 2023

MOUNT VERNON, Ohio (April 7, 2023) — Mount Vernon Nazarene University’s Schnormeier Gallery is proud to present Element – Senior Graphic Design Exhibit from April 11 to April 21, 2023. The exhibition will open on Tuesday, April 11 at 1 p.m. An opening reception for the Seniors will take place at the Gallery from 5:30 - 7:30 p.m., on Friday, April 14, 2023.
